II · Tools Discussed

Beyond the chatbot.

During the masterclass, we moved beyond basic chatbots to explore enterprise-grade workflows and autonomous agents. The primary focus was on Anthropic’s Claude ecosystem, with key comparisons to other market leaders.

  • Claude Desktop App The operating system
    The core of our AI workflow. Unlike the web version, the desktop app allows Claude to interact natively with your computer, execute local tasks, and keep your files secure within local or synced cloud folders — iCloud, Google Drive, and the like.
  • Claude Co-work Deep research & analysis
    A specialized workspace for deep research and complex data analysis. Keeps sensitive client and business data safe while outputting professional documents — PDFs, Excel models, briefs, reports.
  • Claude Design claude.ai/design
    A revolutionary tool that sent shockwaves through the design industry upon launch. Upload your brand guidelines and instantly generate pixel-perfect, exportable LinkedIn ads, marketing mockups, and full presentation decks — no technical design skills required.
  • Connectors The hidden superpower
    Connecting Claude directly to Gmail, Google Drive, Chrome, HubSpot, and even TurboTax automates the mundane. A complete tax return was generated and filed in eighteen minutes during the session.
  • Gemini vs. ChatGPT A brief comparison
    ChatGPT can feel condescending or overly conversational when you just need tasks done. Gemini, on the other hand, is the undisputed king of multimodal inputs — video and images — due to its deep integration with YouTube. More on this in upcoming sessions.

IV · Interactive Q&A

The audience asked.

The audience asked highly insightful questions. A structured summary of the key technical and strategic answers given during the session.

Q.When providing financial documents, do you hide your account numbers to protect data privacy?

Most modern bank statements already mask account numbers to the last four digits. The larger philosophical point: we already trust big tech — Google, Microsoft — with our passwords and financials. With Claude’s desktop environment and Co-work mode, your data and instruction sets remain remarkably localized compared to standard web-based AI tools.

Q.I use Outlook for business and Xero for accounting. How do Connectors work for these?

A connector for Xero is currently in development — a beta was briefly available. Microsoft has restricted read-only access for Outlook to protect its own Copilot tools, meaning Claude can read and draft replies, but you have to copy and paste them manually to send. HubSpot and Gmail, meanwhile, have full read/write integrations.

Q.Should we create separate AI accounts for personal use and business use?

Absolutely. Claude becomes highly personalized to your specific tone, business metrics, and workflow. Keeping personal, family-oriented queries separate from business operations ensures the AI doesn’t cross-contaminate your brand voice.

Q.I have a massive project built over months in ChatGPT. Do I have to start over in Claude?

No. Export your ChatGPT files and code, drop them into a dedicated folder synced to your desktop — iCloud, Google Drive — and point Claude’s Co-work to that folder. Ask Claude to learn the context from those files, and you will pick up exactly where you left off.

Q.Can Claude integrate with DuckDuckGo instead of Chrome for privacy purposes?

Not natively yet. The Chrome integration is required to allow Claude to visually scrape and interact with complex sites like LinkedIn on your behalf. However, Claude only browses Chrome when explicitly directed by your prompt — it does not passively surveil your web traffic.

V · Core Lessons Learnt

A new operational mindset.

Implementing AI is less about learning code and more about adopting a new operational mindset. Keep these four lessons top of mind as you build your own workflows.

Talk to AI like a human, not a search engine.

Avoid rigid prompts like “Act as the Fortune 500 CEO of Pepsi.” That confines the AI to a tiny subset of its data. Instead, give it full context, explain your goal clearly, and let it access its vast knowledge base organically.

Work locally, save globally.

Always use Claude Desktop for deep work, and explicitly assign your Co-work sessions to a synced cloud folder — Google Drive, OneDrive, iCloud. This guarantees you never lose your generated assets, Excel models, or PDF reports if your machine crashes.

Automate the inbox, save the day.

By letting Claude scan your sent items to learn your specific sign-offs and tone, you can set it to auto-draft replies for incoming emails every hour. You retain full control to hit Send — the heavy lifting of composition is eliminated.

The power of integrated design.

With Claude Design, the barrier to entry for high-end graphic creation is gone. You no longer need to fight with alignment in Canva. Simply upload your brand package and ask for a complete LinkedIn campaign or slide deck — in dark and light modes.
